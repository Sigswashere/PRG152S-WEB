Welcome to KeepITBlue

About Us

KeepITBlue is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to cleaning up the major beaches of South Africa. Our mission is to reduce ocean pollution, protect marine life, and create a cleaner, safer environment for everyone to enjoy.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to engage communities in beach cleanups, raise awareness about the impact of plastic pollution, and promote sustainable practices to preserve our oceans for future generations. We believe that every small action can lead to a big change, and together, we can make a difference.
