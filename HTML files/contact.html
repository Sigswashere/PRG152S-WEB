Frequently Asked Questions

What is KeepITBlue?

KeepITBlue is a non-profit organization dedicated to cleaning up the major beaches of South Africa and raising awareness about ocean pollution.

How can I volunteer?

You can volunteer by visiting our volunteer page and filling out the registration form.

How can I donate?

You can donate by visiting our donation page and choosing your preferred payment method.

What do you do with the donations?

Donations are used to organize more cleanups, provide necessary equipment, and raise awareness about ocean pollution.
